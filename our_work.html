PLWD Inclusion

Win Disability Action (WINDA)

Empowering Persons Living with Disabilities

This is a model that aims to empower persons living with disabilities and support workers through capacity building, advocacy, and linkages. We address gaps triggering the resilience of PLWD, including lack of access to quality skill-based education and training, holistic physical environments, and negative attitudes towards PLWD.

EBI supports mobility and rehabilitative devices for physically challenged individuals, ensuring they lead a quality life without intimidation. By doing this, EBI fills the gaps necessary to build a society where PLWD are resilient and empowered.

Skill Up - Step Up Campaign

Empowering Youth & Women Through Skills

This comprehensive initiative provides access to quality education, skill-based training, and employment opportunities for vulnerable youth and women. We equip them with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to realize their potential and become self-reliant, productive members of society.

Our program also includes mentoring, counseling, and networking services to foster both personal and professional economic growth.

📢 NOTE:

The NGO will establish a learning institution – A Skills-Based Training Centre under this program, ensuring long-term empowerment and sustainable development.

Training & Empowerment

Microfinance Empowerment and Development

Economic Empowerment

Breaking the Cycle of Poverty

This microfinance program, implemented by Empower Global Bridge Initiative, aims to offer effective credit and economic empowerment through skill-building and business support.

Through Finance Accelerating Saving Group Transformation (FAST), we target the most vulnerable populations, including women, youth, PLWD, and people living with HIV/AIDS in both rural and urban areas.

Our mission is to partner with like-minded organizations to restore dignity, economic stability, and financial independence, ultimately breaking the cycle of intergenerational poverty.

Healthy Communities Program

Health & Well-being

Promoting Health & Well-being

The Healthy Communities Program is designed to improve the well-being of our communities through health promotion and disease prevention efforts.

We provide health education, awareness, and advocacy on Heath Community, HIV/AIDS, mental health, reproductive health, nutrition, and hygiene, while ensuring access to healthcare services.

Additionally, we work to reduce risk and repacations of age related diseases throght behavioural change such as High blood pressure, Diabities as well as reducing stigma and discrimination and after effects against people living with HIV/AIDS, PLWD, and those with chronic conditions.
